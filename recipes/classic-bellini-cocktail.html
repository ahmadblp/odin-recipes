Classic Bellini Cocktail

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces peach juice
  • 4 fluid ounces chilled sparkling wine
  • 1 tablespoon raspberry juice (Optional)

Prepration

  1. Pour peach juice into a champagne flute. Slowly pour sparkling wine over peach juice; let sit until liquids have blended, about 1 minute. Pour raspberry juice over champagne mixture.
  2. Serve it.

Cook's Note:

Purists do not use Champagne to make this cocktail; it can be too flavorful and hide the subtle peach flavor. If a non-alcoholic cocktail is desired, use sparkling water in place of the sparkling wine.
