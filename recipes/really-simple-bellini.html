Really Simple Bellini Recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can sliced peaches, chilled
  • 1 (750 milliliter) bottle champagne, chilled

Prepration

  1. Blend chilled peaches and their juice in a blender until smooth. Pour into a chilled glass until 1/2 full. Top with champagne; gently stir.
  2. Serve It.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving:101 calories; protein 0.4g; carbohydrates 8.6g; sodium 6.8mg. Full Nutrition
